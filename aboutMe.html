about me

i'm stefano.a web developer

maker and problem solver

Art and technology have always fascinated me, one day it will become my only job.

In 2023 I launched two great challenges to my life, the first great challenge is to become a father, and it's almost time... the second challenge is learning to program, it's a tough but beautiful challenge.
It will take a while but I'm excited for both challenges.

What excites me most in these first experiences is thinking about something and making it happen, or listening to a customer, understanding what they want and being able to make it happen, this is beautiful. This is why sometimes, in addition to developing, I also enjoy learning graphics and marketing concepts. I live this experience and this challenge with this aphorism from giorge bernard

people who progress in life are those who work hard to find the circumstances they want, if they don't find them, they create them

I love this phrase for two reasons, the first reason is this idea of ​​changing my working life and making it smarter, the second reason is a bit the spirit with which I see programming, that is, the idea that something more can always be created effective or more beautiful or more salable.

EXPERIENCE

a great career begins with the first steps, here are my first steps:

  • introductory project to the start2impact web developer course
  • first real project of the start2impact web developer course
  • project created with the current manager of the café owned by my family
  • project created for a basketball association where I played years ago and with which I have collaborated on various projects
  • the first real project commissioned for a B&B chain with a submission deadline of January 2025

SKILL

c++ / HTML / CSS / PHOTOSHOP / ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR / CANVA

I haven't had the opportunity to work in a team yet but I hope to carry out some collaborative projects in the coming months, communication and collaboration are important factors at work
